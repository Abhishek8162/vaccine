VACCINES AVAILABLE

Covishield

The Oxford-AstraZeneca vaccine is being manufactured by the Serum Institute of India, producing more than 60 million doses a month. Its made from a weakened type of a common cold virus .

Covaxin

Bharat Biotech authorised its use after the 2nd phase trials for "unmet medical needs of serious and life-threatening diseases in the country". It was "safe and provides a robust immune response".

Sputnik

Russia's Sputnik V has been deemed to be safe, and works in a way similar to the Oxford-AstraZeneca jab which is being made in India as Covishield. Sputnik V gives around 92% protection against Covid-19 .
